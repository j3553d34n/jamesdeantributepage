James Dean

James Byron Dean was an American actor from Indiana. He is remembered as a cultural icon of teenage disillusionment and social estrangement.

Here you can see a timeline of James Dean his life:

  • Feb. 8, 1931 - James Dean is born in Marion, Indiana to Winton and Mildred Wilson Dean.
  • 1940 - His mother dies of cancer and after that his father sends him to live with quaker relatives in fairmont, Indiana
  • 1949 - He graduates from Fairmont High School in Fairmont, Indiana.
  • 1949 - He moves to California to live with his father, gets a major in drama and his father kicks him out of the house.
  • 1951 - Has a small role in the film Fixed Bayonets.
  • 1952 - Has a role in the film Sailor Beware.
  • 1952 - Has a small role in the film Has Anybody Seen My Gal?
  • 1953 - Has a role in the film Trouble along the Way.
  • 1954 - He appears on a live episode of general electric theater called "The Dark, Dark Hours" with Ronald Reagan.
  • 1955 - Stars in the film East of Eden directed by Elia Kazan.
  • 1955 - Stars in the film Rebel Without a Cause with Nathalie Wood and Sal Mineo
  • 1956 - He dies at the age of 24 after his Porsche 550 spyder collides with another car driven by Cal Poly student Donald Turnupseed near Cholame, California.
